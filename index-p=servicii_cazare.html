Services

Hotel*** Corsa provides:

  • 45 rooms and 3 suites furnished in a modern, air conditioned;
  • bar and pizzeria with terrace and interior garden;
  • breakfast room which can be used as a mini conference (with internet access);
  • 10 m from the beach, private beach in front of the hotel with sun loungers and umbrellas;
  • telephone, refrigerator, TV;
  • bathroom with shower (bath apartments);
  • balcony with table and 2 chairs (in rooms with sea view); city view rooms have not a balcony;
  • non-stop video surveillance parking at reception;
  • reception safe storage levels;
  • free wireless internet in public places;
  • central heating.

The hotel is in an area climatic spa; So is offered spa treatment with highly qualified specialists in sanatoria and Rehabilitation Spa Mangalia.  

You can visit:

  • Arheological Museum Callatis
  • turistic harbour Mangalia
  • Sultan Esmanan mosque
  • "Movila" cave
  • the racecourse
